Valorant:

Valorant is a 5v5 multiplayer online FPS game. It's where 1 team attacks and the other team deffends, it is a serch and destroy type of game. The attacking team trys to plant a bomb and has to detonate it. While the defending team has to try to aviod the bomb and it detonating. Regardless of whether the spike is planted or not, if a squad is wiped out before any other victory condition is met, the opposing squad will win. Matches are around 25 rounds, with each round lasting for 100 seconds.
